# Law of Large Numbers Demo

This demo illustrates the Law of Large Numbers (LLN).

We generate i.i.d. Bernoulli samples with expectation E[X] = 0.3.
As the number of samples increases, the sample mean converges to the true expectation.

The LLN is the foundation of statistical inference:
it guarantees that empirical averages approximate population quantities.

Most machine learning methods rely on this principle,
including empirical risk minimization and parameter estimation.
