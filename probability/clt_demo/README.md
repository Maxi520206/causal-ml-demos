# Central Limit Theorem (CLT) Demo

This demo illustrates the **Central Limit Theorem** using simulation.

The CLT states that the sample mean of independent random variables
converges to a normal distribution as the sample size increases.

Formally:

(X̄ − μ) / (σ / √n) → N(0,1)

## Idea

Even if the original distribution is not normal,
the distribution of the sample mean becomes approximately normal
for large sample sizes.

## Simulation

We simulate samples from a non-normal distribution
and observe how the distribution of the sample mean
approaches a Gaussian.
